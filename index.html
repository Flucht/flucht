Flucht

(ger. "escape")

Data-Headed Look at Why People Flee

        In Europe, the issue of displaced people has received public attention ever since the refugee wave in 2015. While the refugee crisis has existed before, Europe had mostly been unaffected before the defining year. As soon as people had arrived, many figures have exploited the topic to support their own interests. The public discourse became emotional quickly. Facts were neglected and the real misery of displaced people mostly ignored. Read how The Guardian report Patrick Kingsley accurately described the situation in his article “What caused the refugee crisis?” in December 2015.

        Flucht is a data-headed research project for analysing the circumstances which refugees are trying to escape. Nobody is leaving their home under highly precarious circumstances for no reason. We invite everybody to use our Visualisations to learn about the conditions people are trying to escape.
